From initial idea to final product.

Building custom applications and services. Together.

Building robust software can be challenging. Technology advances rapidly, resources and infrastructure can be limited and user requirements may change. ZetaLabs tries to mitigate these problems by taking a customer-first approach and including users in the whole development process to collect feedback early.

Although we are always up-to-date with new technologies, we also value and use proven and robust software that stood the test of time. In particular, we are aware of many battle-tested free and open-source (FOSS) software projects that can help to decrease development time and increase product quality.

Big data? No problem.

Affordable big data processing using elastic infrastructure.

Recently there has been a Cambrian explosion of scientific data availability across different domains. Notably sensor and GIS data is available as open data from several sources which makes it possible to analyze and visualize vast amounts of data to gain new insights into natural or man-made processes.

Different datasets are hosted on different platforms and offer varying protocols and APIs to access them. ZetaLabs will help you in accessing, processing and refining this data by merging your existing domain-specific knowledge with our understanding utilizing public or private compute capabilities.
